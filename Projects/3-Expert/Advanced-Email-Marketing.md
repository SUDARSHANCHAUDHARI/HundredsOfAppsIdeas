# Advanced Email Marketing

## Description
An advanced email marketing platform that develops sophisticated solutions for email campaign management, personalization, automation, and analytics for effective email marketing strategies.

## User Stories
- As an email marketer, I want comprehensive campaign tools
- As a business owner, I want email automation capabilities
- As a designer, I want intuitive email template builders
- As an analyst, I want email performance analytics
- As a subscriber, I want personalized email experiences

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered email personalization
- Automated email workflows and sequences
- Advanced segmentation and targeting
- A/B testing and optimization
- Email deliverability monitoring
- Interactive email content
- Multi-channel integration
- Compliance and privacy management
