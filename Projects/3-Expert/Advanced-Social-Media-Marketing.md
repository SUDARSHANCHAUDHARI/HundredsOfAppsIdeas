# Advanced Social Media Marketing

## Description
An advanced social media marketing platform that develops sophisticated solutions for social media management, content scheduling, analytics, and multi-channel social media marketing strategies.

## User Stories
- As a social media manager, I want comprehensive management tools
- As a marketer, I want social media analytics and insights
- As a content creator, I want scheduling and automation
- As a business owner, I want social media ROI tracking
- As an analyst, I want engagement and performance metrics

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered content recommendations
- Multi-platform social media management
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- Social listening and sentiment analysis
- Influencer management and tracking
- Automated content scheduling
- Social media advertising integration
- Cross-platform performance tracking
