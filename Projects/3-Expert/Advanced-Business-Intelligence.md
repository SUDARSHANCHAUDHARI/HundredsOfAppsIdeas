# Advanced Business Intelligence

## Description
An advanced business intelligence platform that develops sophisticated BI solutions for data visualization, reporting, analytics, and strategic business decision making across enterprise operations.

## User Stories
- As a BI analyst, I want comprehensive analytics tools
- As a business manager, I want actionable insights from data
- As an executive, I want strategic dashboards and reports
- As a data analyst, I want advanced visualization capabilities
- As a stakeholder, I want real-time business metrics

## Bonus Features
- Interactive dashboards and reports
- Real-time data visualization
- Predictive analytics and forecasting
- Self-service BI capabilities
- Mobile BI and reporting
- Data storytelling and insights
- Performance metrics and KPIs
- Cross-functional analytics
