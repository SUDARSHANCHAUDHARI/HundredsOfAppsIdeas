# Synthetic Biology Platform

## Description
An advanced synthetic biology platform that enables the design, engineering, and programming of biological systems for medical, environmental, and industrial applications using DNA synthesis and genetic engineering.

## User Stories
- As a synthetic biologist, I want to design and program biological systems
- As a medical researcher, I want to develop synthetic organisms for therapy
- As an environmental engineer, I want to create bio-based solutions
- As a biotech entrepreneur, I want to develop synthetic biology products
- As a student, I want to learn genetic engineering and synthetic biology

## Bonus Features
- DNA sequence design and optimization
- Genetic circuit engineering
- Synthetic organism modeling
- Automated DNA synthesis and assembly
- Bio-safety and containment systems
- Metabolic pathway engineering
- Protein design and engineering
- Integration with biomanufacturing
