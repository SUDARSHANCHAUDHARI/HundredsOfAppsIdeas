# Climate Modeling System

## Description
An advanced climate modeling and prediction system that uses AI and supercomputing to simulate Earth's climate, predict future changes, and model the impact of climate interventions.

## User Stories
- As a climate scientist, I want to run complex climate simulations
- As a policy maker, I want to understand climate change impacts
- As an environmental researcher, I want to model intervention strategies
- As an energy company, I want to plan for climate-related risks
- As a farmer, I want to receive localized climate predictions

## Bonus Features
- High-resolution climate simulation and modeling
- AI-enhanced climate prediction accuracy
- Real-time climate data integration from satellites
- Climate intervention scenario modeling
- Regional and local climate impact assessment
- Extreme weather event prediction
- Carbon cycle and ecosystem modeling
- Climate policy impact analysis tools

## Technologies to Consider
- Supercomputing: HPC clusters, GPU acceleration, distributed computing
- AI/ML: Climate pattern recognition, predictive modeling
- Data Sources: Satellite data, weather stations, ocean buoys
- Simulation: Numerical weather prediction, climate models
- Visualization: Climate data visualization, interactive maps
- Cloud Computing: Scalable climate data processing
- Scientific Computing: Climate modeling frameworks, numerical methods
- Integration: Environmental APIs, satellite data feeds
