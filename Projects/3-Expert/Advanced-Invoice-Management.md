# Advanced Invoice Management

## Description
An advanced invoice management platform that provides comprehensive solutions for creating, sending, tracking, and managing invoices with automated billing and payment processing.

## User Stories
- As a business owner, I want to create and send invoices
- As an accountant, I want to track payments and receivables
- As a customer, I want to view and pay invoices
- As an admin, I want to manage billing workflows
- As a developer, I want to integrate invoice features

## Bonus Features
- Invoice creation and templates
- Automated billing and recurring invoices
- Payment processing and tracking
- Invoice analytics and reporting
- Integration with accounting systems
- Mobile invoice management
- Automated reminders and follow-ups
- Multi-currency and tax support

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for invoice interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, PHP for invoice API
- Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL for invoice data
- Payments: Stripe, PayPal, Square integration
- Documents: PDF generation, invoice templates
- Analytics: Invoice metrics, payment tracking
- Integration: Accounting, CRM systems
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for invoice apps
