# Advanced Food Delivery

## Description
An advanced food delivery platform that provides comprehensive solutions for restaurant management, order processing, delivery tracking, and customer experience optimization for food delivery businesses.

## User Stories
- As a restaurant owner, I want to manage orders and delivery
- As a customer, I want to order food easily and track delivery
- As a delivery driver, I want to manage deliveries efficiently
- As a platform admin, I want to coordinate all participants
- As a developer, I want to integrate food delivery features

## Bonus Features
- Restaurant management and menu systems
- Order processing and payment integration
- Delivery tracking and route optimization
- Customer loyalty and rewards programs
- Real-time notifications and updates
- Mobile food delivery apps
- Integration with payment processors
- Analytics and reporting

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for food delivery interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, Go for delivery API
- Database: PostgreSQL, MongoDB for delivery data
- Maps: Google Maps, Mapbox for delivery tracking
- Payments: Stripe, PayPal for payment processing
- Real-time: WebSocket for order updates
- Analytics: Delivery metrics, customer insights
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for delivery apps
