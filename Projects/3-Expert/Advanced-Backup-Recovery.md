# Advanced Backup Recovery

## Description
An advanced backup and recovery platform that develops sophisticated solutions for data protection, disaster recovery, business continuity, and comprehensive backup management across hybrid environments.

## User Stories
- As a backup administrator, I want comprehensive backup tools
- As a disaster recovery manager, I want recovery automation
- As a business owner, I want business continuity assurance
- As a compliance officer, I want backup compliance
- As an analyst, I want backup performance metrics

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered backup optimization and scheduling
- Automated disaster recovery and failover
- Multi-site backup and replication
- Business continuity planning and testing
- Backup compliance and audit support
- Integration with storage and cloud systems
- Ransomware protection and recovery
- Cost optimization and capacity planning
