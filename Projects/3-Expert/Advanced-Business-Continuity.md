# Advanced Business Continuity

## Description
An advanced business continuity platform that develops sophisticated solutions for continuity planning, risk assessment, emergency management, and comprehensive business resilience strategies.

## User Stories
- As a continuity planner, I want comprehensive continuity tools
- As a risk manager, I want business resilience assessment
- As an emergency manager, I want crisis response capabilities
- As a business owner, I want continuity assurance
- As a stakeholder, I want resilience visibility

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered risk assessment and mitigation
- Automated continuity planning and testing
- Emergency response coordination and management
- Business resilience optimization
- Multi-site continuity strategies
- Integration with disaster recovery systems
- Compliance and audit support
- Cost optimization and ROI analysis
