# Fusion Energy System

## Description
An advanced fusion energy research and development platform that simulates, designs, and optimizes nuclear fusion reactors for clean, sustainable energy production.

## User Stories
- As a fusion researcher, I want to simulate plasma physics and fusion reactions
- As an energy engineer, I want to design efficient fusion reactor systems
- As a policy maker, I want to understand fusion energy viability
- As a student, I want to learn about fusion energy principles
- As an investor, I want to evaluate fusion energy opportunities

## Bonus Features
- Plasma physics simulation and modeling
- Magnetic confinement fusion design
- Inertial confinement fusion optimization
- Fusion materials science research
- Tritium breeding and fuel cycle management
- Fusion reactor safety analysis
- Energy output and efficiency optimization
- Integration with power grid systems

## Technologies to Consider
- Physics Simulation: Plasma physics, particle physics, fluid dynamics
- Supercomputing: HPC clusters, GPU acceleration for fusion modeling
- Materials Science: High-temperature materials, radiation-resistant alloys
- Control Systems: Real-time plasma control, magnetic field management
- AI/ML: Fusion optimization, plasma behavior prediction
- Engineering: Reactor design, thermal management, vacuum systems
- Safety: Radiation shielding, emergency systems
- Energy: Power conversion, grid integration, energy storage
