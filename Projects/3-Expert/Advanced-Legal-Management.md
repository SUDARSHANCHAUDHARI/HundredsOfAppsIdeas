# Advanced Legal Management

## Description
An advanced legal management platform that provides comprehensive solutions for case management, document automation, client management, and billing for law firms and legal professionals.

## User Stories
- As a lawyer, I want to manage cases and documents efficiently
- As a paralegal, I want to organize legal documents and research
- As a law firm administrator, I want to track billing and productivity
- As a client, I want to track case progress and communicate
- As a developer, I want to integrate legal features

## Bonus Features
- Case management and tracking
- Document automation and templates
- Client relationship management
- Time tracking and billing
- Legal research integration
- Mobile legal management
- Integration with court systems
- Compliance and deadline tracking

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for legal interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, PHP for legal API
- Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL for legal data
- Documents: PDF generation, document automation
- Integration: Legal research, court systems
- Security: Attorney-client privilege, compliance
- Analytics: Case metrics, billing tracking
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for legal apps
