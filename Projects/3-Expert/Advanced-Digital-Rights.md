# Advanced Digital Rights

## Description
An advanced digital rights management platform that develops sophisticated solutions for content protection, licensing management, copyright enforcement, and comprehensive digital rights automation.

## User Stories
- As a rights manager, I want comprehensive DRM tools
- As a content creator, I want copyright protection
- As a publisher, I want licensing management
- As a legal professional, I want rights enforcement
- As a business owner, I want content security

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered rights monitoring and enforcement
- Automated licensing and royalty management
- Digital content protection and encryption
- Copyright infringement detection
- Multi-platform rights management
- Integration with content distribution systems
- Compliance and regulatory support
- Cost optimization and revenue tracking
