# Advanced Car Wash

## Description
An advanced car wash platform that provides comprehensive solutions for car wash service management, scheduling, customer loyalty, and business operations for car wash businesses and franchises.

## User Stories
- As a car wash owner, I want to manage services and customers efficiently
- As a customer, I want to book car wash services and track my visits
- As a service manager, I want to optimize wash schedules and staff
- As a franchise owner, I want to manage multiple locations
- As a developer, I want to integrate car wash features

## Bonus Features
- Service scheduling and booking
- Customer loyalty and membership programs
- Wash package management and pricing
- Staff scheduling and performance tracking
- Inventory and supply management
- Mobile car wash apps
- Integration with payment processors
- Analytics and reporting

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for car wash interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, PHP for car wash API
- Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL for car wash data
- Scheduling: Calendar integration, optimization
- Payments: Stripe, PayPal for payment processing
- Analytics: Service metrics, customer insights
- Integration: Payment processors, loyalty systems
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for car wash apps
