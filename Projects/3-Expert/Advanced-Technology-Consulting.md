# Advanced Technology Consulting

## Description
An advanced technology consulting platform that provides strategic technology guidance, digital transformation solutions, and innovation consulting for businesses adopting cutting-edge technologies.

## User Stories
- As a technology consultant, I want comprehensive consulting tools
- As a business leader, I want strategic technology guidance
- As a CTO, I want technology roadmap planning
- As a project manager, I want implementation guidance
- As an innovator, I want emerging technology insights

## Bonus Features
- Technology assessment and roadmap
- Digital transformation strategy
- Innovation consulting and ideation
- Technology stack optimization
- Vendor evaluation and selection
- Change management and training
- ROI analysis and metrics
- Industry-specific solutions
