# Advanced Music Platform

## Description
An advanced music platform that provides comprehensive solutions for music streaming, distribution, artist management, and music discovery with monetization and analytics.

## User Stories
- As a musician, I want to upload and distribute my music
- As a listener, I want to discover and stream music
- As a label, I want to manage artists and catalogs
- As a business owner, I want to monetize music content
- As a developer, I want to integrate music features

## Bonus Features
- Music upload and transcoding
- Streaming music player with playlists
- Music discovery and recommendations
- Artist profiles and management
- Music monetization and licensing
- Music analytics and insights
- Radio and curated playlists
- Mobile apps for music streaming

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for music interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, Go for API
- Audio Processing: FFmpeg, Librosa for processing
- Streaming: HLS, DASH for music streaming
- Storage: AWS S3, Google Cloud Storage
- CDN: CloudFlare, AWS CloudFront for delivery
- Database: PostgreSQL, MongoDB for music data
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for music apps
