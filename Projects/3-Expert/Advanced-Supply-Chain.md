# Advanced Supply Chain

## Description
An advanced supply chain management platform that develops sophisticated solutions for inventory management, logistics optimization, demand forecasting, and end-to-end supply chain visibility.

## User Stories
- As a supply chain manager, I want comprehensive visibility tools
- As a logistics coordinator, I want route optimization
- As an inventory manager, I want predictive stock management
- As a business owner, I want cost optimization insights
- As an analyst, I want supply chain analytics

## Bonus Features
- Real-time supply chain visibility
- AI-powered demand forecasting
- Inventory optimization and management
- Route optimization and logistics planning
- Supplier relationship management
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Sustainability and compliance tracking
- Integration with ERP systems
