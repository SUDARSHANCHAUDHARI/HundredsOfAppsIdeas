# Space Based Solar Power

## Description
An advanced space-based solar power platform that designs, simulates, and optimizes satellite solar power systems that collect solar energy in space and transmit it wirelessly to Earth for clean energy generation.

## User Stories
- As an energy engineer, I want to design space-based solar power systems
- As a space agency, I want to develop orbital power stations
- As an environmental researcher, I want to study clean energy solutions
- As a policy maker, I want to evaluate space power feasibility
- As an investor, I want to explore space energy opportunities

## Bonus Features
- Orbital solar panel design and optimization
- Wireless power transmission systems
- Energy beam steering and targeting
- Space debris avoidance and safety systems
- Ground-based receiver station design
- Energy conversion and grid integration
- Economic viability analysis
- Environmental impact assessment
