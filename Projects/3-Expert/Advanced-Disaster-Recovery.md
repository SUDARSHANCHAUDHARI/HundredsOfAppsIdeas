# Advanced Disaster Recovery

## Description
An advanced disaster recovery platform that develops sophisticated solutions for business continuity, emergency response, system restoration, and comprehensive disaster recovery planning and testing.

## User Stories
- As a disaster recovery manager, I want comprehensive DR tools
- As a business continuity planner, I want continuity assurance
- As an IT manager, I want system restoration capabilities
- As a compliance officer, I want DR compliance
- As a stakeholder, I want recovery assurance

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered disaster prediction and planning
- Automated system restoration and failover
- Business continuity testing and validation
- Emergency response coordination
- Multi-site disaster recovery
- Integration with backup and storage systems
- Compliance and audit support
- Cost optimization and risk assessment
