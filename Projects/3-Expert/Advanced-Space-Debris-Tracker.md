# Advanced Space Debris Tracker

## Description
A sophisticated space debris tracking and management system that monitors orbital objects, predicts collision risks, and provides solutions for space sustainability using advanced orbital mechanics and AI.

## User Stories
- As a space agency, I want to track and predict space debris movements
- As a satellite operator, I want collision avoidance recommendations
- As a researcher, I want to study space debris accumulation patterns
- As a policy maker, I want to understand space sustainability challenges
- As an aerospace engineer, I want to design debris mitigation solutions

## Bonus Features
- Real-time orbital object tracking
- AI-powered collision prediction algorithms
- Space debris simulation and modeling
- Integration with satellite tracking systems
- Automated collision avoidance recommendations
- Space sustainability assessment tools
- 3D visualization of orbital environments
- Integration with international space databases
