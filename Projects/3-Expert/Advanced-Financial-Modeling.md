# Advanced Financial Modeling Platform

## Description
A sophisticated financial modeling platform that simulates complex financial instruments, predicts market behavior, and provides risk analysis using quantitative finance and machine learning techniques.

## User Stories
- As a quantitative analyst, I want to model complex financial instruments
- As a trader, I want to predict market movements and risks
- As a risk manager, I want to analyze portfolio risk exposure
- As a researcher, I want to test trading strategies
- As a student, I want to learn quantitative finance concepts

## Bonus Features
- Monte Carlo simulation for financial modeling
- AI-powered market prediction algorithms
- Real-time market data integration
- Complex derivatives pricing models
- Risk management and portfolio optimization
- Algorithmic trading strategy testing
- Financial time series analysis
- Integration with major exchanges
