# Advanced Customer Support

## Description
An advanced customer support platform that develops sophisticated solutions for customer service automation, omnichannel support, knowledge management, and customer experience optimization.

## User Stories
- As a support agent, I want comprehensive support tools
- As a customer, I want fast and effective support
- As a manager, I want support analytics and insights
- As a business owner, I want customer satisfaction optimization
- As an analyst, I want support performance metrics

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered ticket routing and prioritization
- Omnichannel support integration
- Knowledge base and self-service
- Real-time chat and video support
- Customer satisfaction monitoring
- Support analytics and reporting
- Automated responses and workflows
- Integration with CRM and business systems
