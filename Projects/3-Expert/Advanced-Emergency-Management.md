# Advanced Emergency Management

## Description
An advanced emergency management platform that develops sophisticated solutions for emergency response coordination, crisis management, incident tracking, and comprehensive emergency preparedness planning.

## User Stories
- As an emergency manager, I want comprehensive emergency tools
- As a crisis coordinator, I want response coordination
- As a first responder, I want incident tracking
- As a business owner, I want emergency preparedness
- As a stakeholder, I want emergency visibility

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered emergency prediction and planning
- Real-time emergency response coordination
- Crisis management and communication
- Multi-agency emergency integration
- Emergency resource management
- Integration with public safety systems
- Compliance and regulatory support
- Cost optimization and resource planning
