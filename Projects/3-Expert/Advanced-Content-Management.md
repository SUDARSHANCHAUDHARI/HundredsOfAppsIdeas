# Advanced Content Management

## Description
An advanced content management platform that develops sophisticated CMS solutions for content creation, management, personalization, and multi-channel content delivery across digital experiences.

## User Stories
- As a content manager, I want comprehensive content tools
- As a marketer, I want content personalization capabilities
- As a developer, I want flexible content APIs
- As an editor, I want intuitive content creation tools
- As an administrator, I want content governance features

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered content personalization
- Multi-channel content delivery
- Content workflow and approval processes
- SEO optimization and analytics
- Content performance tracking
- Headless CMS architecture
- Multi-language content management
- Integration with marketing automation
