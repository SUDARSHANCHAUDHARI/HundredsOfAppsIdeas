# Quantum Internet

## Description
An advanced quantum internet platform that develops quantum communication networks, quantum repeaters, and quantum routing protocols to enable secure, ultra-fast quantum information transmission between quantum computers and devices.

## User Stories
- As a quantum researcher, I want to build quantum communication networks
- As a network engineer, I want to design quantum routing protocols
- As a security expert, I want unhackable quantum communication
- As a physicist, I want to study quantum entanglement distribution
- As a developer, I want to create quantum network applications

## Bonus Features
- Quantum entanglement distribution
- Quantum repeater technology
- Quantum routing and switching
- Quantum error correction for networks
- Quantum key distribution networks
- Quantum teleportation protocols
- Hybrid quantum-classical networking
- Integration with quantum computers
