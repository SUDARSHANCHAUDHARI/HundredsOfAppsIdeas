# Advanced Audio Platform

## Description
An advanced audio platform that provides comprehensive solutions for audio hosting, streaming, podcasting, and music distribution with monetization and analytics.

## User Stories
- As a content creator, I want to upload and share audio content
- As a listener, I want to discover and stream audio
- As a podcaster, I want to distribute and monetize podcasts
- As a musician, I want to share and sell music
- As an admin, I want to manage audio content and users

## Bonus Features
- Audio upload and transcoding
- Streaming audio player with playlists
- Podcast hosting and distribution
- Music monetization and licensing
- Audio analytics and insights
- Live audio streaming
- Content management and moderation
- Mobile apps for audio consumption

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for audio interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, Go for API
- Audio Processing: FFmpeg, Librosa for processing
- Streaming: HLS, DASH for audio streaming
- Storage: AWS S3, Google Cloud Storage
- CDN: CloudFlare, AWS CloudFront for delivery
- Database: PostgreSQL, MongoDB for metadata
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for mobile apps
