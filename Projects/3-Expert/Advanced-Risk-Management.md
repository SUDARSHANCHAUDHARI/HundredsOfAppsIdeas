# Advanced Risk Management

## Description
An advanced risk management platform that develops sophisticated solutions for risk assessment, mitigation strategies, threat intelligence, and comprehensive risk monitoring across business operations.

## User Stories
- As a risk manager, I want comprehensive risk tools
- As a business owner, I want risk mitigation strategies
- As an analyst, I want risk assessment and analytics
- As a compliance officer, I want risk reporting
- As a stakeholder, I want risk visibility

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered risk assessment and prediction
- Real-time risk monitoring and alerts
- Risk mitigation strategy development
- Threat intelligence and analysis
- Risk reporting and analytics
- Integration with business systems
- Compliance and regulatory risk management
- Scenario analysis and stress testing
