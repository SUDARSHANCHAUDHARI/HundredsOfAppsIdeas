# Advanced Fintech

## Description
An advanced fintech platform that develops sophisticated financial technology solutions including digital banking, investment platforms, payment systems, and blockchain-based financial services.

## User Stories
- As a fintech developer, I want to build advanced financial applications
- As a financial institution, I want modern digital banking solutions
- As an investor, I want sophisticated trading and investment tools
- As a business owner, I want integrated payment solutions
- As a regulator, I want compliant and secure financial systems

## Bonus Features
- Digital banking and mobile payments
- Algorithmic trading systems
- Robo-advisory services
- Blockchain and cryptocurrency integration
- Risk management and compliance
- Real-time market data processing
- Advanced security and fraud detection
- Multi-currency and international payments
