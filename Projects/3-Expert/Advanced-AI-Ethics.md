# Advanced AI Ethics

## Description
An advanced AI ethics platform that develops, tests, and implements ethical frameworks for artificial intelligence, including bias detection, fairness algorithms, transparency tools, and ethical decision-making systems.

## User Stories
- As an AI developer, I want to ensure my AI systems are ethical
- As a policy maker, I want to regulate AI ethics and safety
- As a researcher, I want to study AI bias and fairness
- As a business leader, I want to implement responsible AI
- As a citizen, I want to understand AI ethical implications

## Bonus Features
- AI bias detection and mitigation
- Fairness algorithm development
- Explainable AI tools and frameworks
- Ethical decision-making systems
- AI transparency and interpretability
- Privacy-preserving AI techniques
- AI safety and alignment research
- Regulatory compliance automation
