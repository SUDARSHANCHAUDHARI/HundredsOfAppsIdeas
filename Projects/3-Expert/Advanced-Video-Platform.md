# Advanced Video Platform

## Description
An advanced video platform that provides comprehensive solutions for video hosting, streaming, monetization, and analytics for content creators and businesses.

## User Stories
- As a content creator, I want to upload and share videos
- As a viewer, I want to watch and discover videos
- As a business owner, I want to monetize video content
- As an admin, I want to manage video content and users
- As a developer, I want to integrate video features

## Bonus Features
- Video upload and transcoding
- Adaptive streaming and video player
- Video monetization and subscriptions
- Video analytics and insights
- Live streaming capabilities
- Video recommendations and discovery
- Content management and moderation
- Mobile apps for video consumption

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for video interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, Go for API
- Video Processing: FFmpeg, AWS Elemental
- Streaming: HLS, DASH for adaptive streaming
- Storage: AWS S3, Google Cloud Storage
- CDN: CloudFlare, AWS CloudFront for delivery
- Database: PostgreSQL, MongoDB for metadata
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for mobile apps
