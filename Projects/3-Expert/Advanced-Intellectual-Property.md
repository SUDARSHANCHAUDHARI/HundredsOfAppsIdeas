# Advanced Intellectual Property

## Description
An advanced intellectual property platform that develops sophisticated solutions for IP management, patent tracking, trademark protection, and comprehensive intellectual property portfolio management.

## User Stories
- As an IP manager, I want comprehensive IP tools
- As a patent attorney, I want patent tracking
- As a trademark manager, I want brand protection
- As a legal professional, I want IP enforcement
- As a business owner, I want IP asset management

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered IP portfolio optimization
- Automated patent and trademark monitoring
- Intellectual property valuation and analysis
- Multi-jurisdiction IP protection
- Integration with legal and compliance systems
- IP licensing and royalty management
- Compliance and regulatory support
- Cost optimization and revenue tracking
