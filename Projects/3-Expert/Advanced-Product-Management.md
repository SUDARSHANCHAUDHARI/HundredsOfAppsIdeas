# Advanced Product Management

## Description
An advanced product management platform that develops sophisticated solutions for product strategy, roadmap planning, user research, and product lifecycle management with data-driven insights.

## User Stories
- As a product manager, I want comprehensive product tools
- As a product owner, I want roadmap and feature management
- As a user researcher, I want user feedback and insights
- As a stakeholder, I want product performance metrics
- As a developer, I want clear product requirements

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered product insights and recommendations
- Product roadmap and feature prioritization
- User research and feedback analysis
- Product analytics and performance tracking
- Competitive analysis and market insights
- Product lifecycle management
- Integration with development and business systems
- Stakeholder collaboration and communication
