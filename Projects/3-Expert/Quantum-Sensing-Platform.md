# Quantum Sensing Platform

## Description
An advanced quantum sensing platform that leverages quantum phenomena to develop ultra-precise sensors for medical imaging, navigation, materials analysis, and fundamental physics research.

## User Stories
- As a researcher, I want to develop ultra-sensitive quantum sensors
- As a medical professional, I want advanced quantum imaging for diagnosis
- As a navigator, I want quantum-enhanced positioning systems
- As a materials scientist, I want quantum-level material analysis
- As a physicist, I want to explore fundamental quantum phenomena

## Bonus Features
- Quantum-enhanced magnetic field sensing
- Atomic clock and time measurement
- Quantum gravimetry and inertial sensing
- Single-photon detection and imaging
- Quantum radar and lidar systems
- NV-center based quantum sensors
- Quantum thermometry and pressure sensing
- Integration with classical sensing systems
