# Advanced E-commerce

## Description
An advanced e-commerce platform that builds sophisticated online shopping experiences with AI-powered recommendations, real-time inventory management, and comprehensive payment systems.

## User Stories
- As an e-commerce developer, I want to build advanced shopping platforms
- As a business owner, I want to increase sales and customer engagement
- As a customer, I want personalized shopping experiences
- As a marketer, I want advanced analytics and customer insights
- As an administrator, I want comprehensive management tools

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered product recommendations
- Real-time inventory management
- Advanced search and filtering
- Multi-vendor marketplace
- Dynamic pricing and promotions
- Customer behavior analytics
- Omnichannel integration
- Advanced payment processing

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js, Next.js
- Backend: Node.js, Python, Java
- Databases: PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis
- Search: Elasticsearch, Algolia
- Payments: Stripe, PayPal, Square
- Analytics: Google Analytics, Mixpanel
- Inventory: Real-time stock management
- Security: PCI compliance, fraud detection
