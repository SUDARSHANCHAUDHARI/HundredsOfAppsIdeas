# Advanced Customer Relationship

## Description
An advanced customer relationship management platform that develops sophisticated CRM solutions with AI-powered insights, personalized experiences, and comprehensive customer lifecycle management.

## User Stories
- As a sales manager, I want comprehensive customer insights
- As a marketing professional, I want customer segmentation tools
- As a customer service rep, I want unified customer information
- As a business owner, I want customer retention strategies
- As an analyst, I want customer behavior analytics

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered customer insights and predictions
- 360-degree customer view
- Personalized customer experiences
- Automated lead scoring and routing
- Customer journey mapping and optimization
- Real-time customer analytics
- Multi-channel communication management
- Integration with sales and marketing tools
