# Advanced Tax Management

## Description
An advanced tax management platform that provides comprehensive solutions for tax calculation, filing, compliance, and planning for businesses and individuals.

## User Stories
- As a business owner, I want to manage tax compliance
- As an accountant, I want to prepare and file taxes
- As a tax professional, I want to manage client taxes
- As an admin, I want to ensure tax compliance
- As a developer, I want to integrate tax features

## Bonus Features
- Automated tax calculation and filing
- Tax compliance monitoring and alerts
- Tax planning and optimization tools
- Multi-jurisdiction tax support
- Integration with accounting systems
- Tax document management
- Tax analytics and reporting
- Mobile tax management

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for tax interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, PHP for tax API
- Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL for tax data
- Integration: Accounting, ERP systems
- Security: Tax data protection, compliance
- Analytics: Tax metrics, compliance tracking
- Real-time: Tax rate updates, compliance alerts
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for tax apps
