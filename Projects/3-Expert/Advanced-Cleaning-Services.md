# Advanced Cleaning Services

## Description
An advanced cleaning services platform that provides comprehensive solutions for cleaning business management, scheduling, customer communication, and service quality tracking for residential and commercial cleaning companies.

## User Stories
- As a cleaning business owner, I want to manage services and staff
- As a customer, I want to book cleaning services easily
- As a cleaner, I want to manage my schedule and tasks
- As an admin, I want to track business performance
- As a developer, I want to integrate cleaning features

## Bonus Features
- Service booking and scheduling
- Staff management and assignment
- Customer relationship management
- Quality control and feedback
- Inventory and supply management
- Mobile cleaning apps
- Integration with payment processors
- Analytics and reporting

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for cleaning interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, PHP for cleaning API
- Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL for cleaning data
- Scheduling: Calendar integration, optimization
- Payments: Stripe, PayPal for payment processing
- Analytics: Service metrics, quality tracking
- Integration: Payment processors, communication tools
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for cleaning apps
