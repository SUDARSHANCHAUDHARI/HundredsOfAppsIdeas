# Advanced Benefits Administration

## Description
An advanced benefits administration platform that provides comprehensive solutions for managing employee benefits, enrollment, and compliance with health insurance and retirement plans.

## User Stories
- As an HR manager, I want to manage employee benefits
- As an employee, I want to enroll in and manage my benefits
- As a business owner, I want to offer competitive benefits
- As a benefits broker, I want to manage client benefits
- As a developer, I want to integrate benefits features

## Bonus Features
- Benefits enrollment and management
- Health insurance administration
- Retirement plan management
- Compliance monitoring and reporting
- Employee self-service portal
- Benefits analytics and insights
- Integration with payroll systems
- Mobile benefits access

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for benefits interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, PHP for benefits API
- Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL for benefits data
- Integration: Insurance carriers, payroll systems
- Security: Benefits data protection, compliance
- Analytics: Benefits utilization, cost tracking
- Real-time: Enrollment processing, compliance alerts
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for benefits apps
