# Advanced Contract Management

## Description
An advanced contract management platform that provides comprehensive solutions for creating, storing, tracking, and managing contracts with automated workflows and compliance features.

## User Stories
- As a legal professional, I want to manage contracts efficiently
- As a business owner, I want to track contract obligations
- As a sales manager, I want to manage sales contracts
- As an admin, I want to ensure contract compliance
- As a developer, I want to integrate contract features

## Bonus Features
- Contract creation and templates
- Digital signatures and approvals
- Contract lifecycle management
- Automated renewal and expiration alerts
- Compliance and audit tracking
- Integration with legal and business systems
- Mobile contract access and management
- Contract analytics and reporting

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for contract interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, PHP for contract API
- Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL for contract data
- Documents: PDF generation, template engines
- Signatures: Electronic signature integration
- Security: Contract encryption, access control
- Integration: Legal, CRM, ERP systems
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for contract apps
