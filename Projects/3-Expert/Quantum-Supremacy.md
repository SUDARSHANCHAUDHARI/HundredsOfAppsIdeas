# Quantum Supremacy

## Description
An advanced quantum supremacy platform that develops and demonstrates quantum computers capable of solving problems that are intractable for classical computers, pushing the boundaries of computational capability.

## User Stories
- As a quantum researcher, I want to achieve quantum supremacy demonstrations
- As a computer scientist, I want to solve classically intractable problems
- As an algorithm developer, I want to create quantum advantage algorithms
- As a hardware engineer, I want to build quantum supremacy systems
- As a theorist, I want to understand quantum computational complexity

## Bonus Features
- Quantum supremacy experiment design
- Quantum advantage algorithm development
- Quantum error correction implementation
- Quantum benchmarking and validation
- Classical-quantum complexity comparison
- Quantum optimization problem solving
- Quantum simulation of complex systems
- Integration with classical computing
