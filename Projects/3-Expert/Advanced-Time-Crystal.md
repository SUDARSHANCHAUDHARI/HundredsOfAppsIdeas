# Advanced Time Crystal

## Description
An advanced time crystal research platform that develops, simulates, and studies time crystals - quantum systems that break time-translation symmetry - for quantum computing, metrology, and fundamental physics research.

## User Stories
- As a quantum physicist, I want to create and study time crystals
- As a researcher, I want to explore non-equilibrium quantum phases
- As a metrologist, I want to develop time crystal-based sensors
- As a quantum engineer, I want to use time crystals for quantum computing
- As a student, I want to understand time crystal physics

## Bonus Features
- Time crystal creation and stabilization
- Non-equilibrium quantum phase simulation
- Time crystal-based quantum memory
- Precision time measurement applications
- Quantum sensing with time crystals
- Many-body localization studies
- Floquet engineering techniques
- Integration with quantum processors
