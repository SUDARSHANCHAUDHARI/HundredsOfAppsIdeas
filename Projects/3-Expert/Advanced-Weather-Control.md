# Advanced Weather Control

## Description
An advanced weather control and modification platform that researches, simulates, and develops technologies for weather modification, climate engineering, and atmospheric intervention for environmental and agricultural applications.

## User Stories
- As a climate scientist, I want to study weather modification techniques
- As an agricultural engineer, I want to control weather for crop optimization
- As an environmental researcher, I want to explore climate engineering
- As a disaster manager, I want to prevent extreme weather events
- As a policy maker, I want to understand weather control implications

## Bonus Features
- Cloud seeding and precipitation control
- Atmospheric modeling and simulation
- Climate engineering technologies
- Weather pattern modification
- Drought prevention and mitigation
- Storm dissipation and control
- Agricultural weather optimization
- Environmental impact assessment
