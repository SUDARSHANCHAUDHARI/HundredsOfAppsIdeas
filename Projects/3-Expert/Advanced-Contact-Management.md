# Advanced Contact Management

## Description
An advanced contact management platform that provides comprehensive solutions for organizing, tracking, and managing customer and business contacts with relationship management features.

## User Stories
- As a sales professional, I want to manage my contacts efficiently
- As a business owner, I want to track customer interactions
- As a marketer, I want to segment contacts for campaigns
- As an admin, I want to manage contact data and permissions
- As a developer, I want to integrate contact features

## Bonus Features
- Contact database and organization
- Interaction tracking and history
- Contact segmentation and tagging
- Email integration and synchronization
- Relationship mapping and visualization
- Contact analytics and insights
- Mobile contact management
- Integration with CRM systems

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for contact interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, PHP for contact API
- Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL for contact data
- Email: Email integration, synchronization
- Analytics: Contact engagement, interaction tracking
- Integration: REST APIs for third-party services
- Security: Data encryption, access control
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for contact apps
