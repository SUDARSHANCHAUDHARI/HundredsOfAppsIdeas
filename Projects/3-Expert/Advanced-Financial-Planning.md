# Advanced Financial Planning

## Description
An advanced financial planning platform that develops sophisticated solutions for budgeting, forecasting, investment planning, and financial analytics for personal and business finance management.

## User Stories
- As a financial planner, I want comprehensive planning tools
- As an investor, I want portfolio management insights
- As a business owner, I want financial analytics and forecasting
- As an individual, I want personal finance management
- As an analyst, I want financial modeling capabilities

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered financial forecasting
- Portfolio optimization and management
- Budget planning and expense tracking
- Investment analysis and recommendations
- Risk assessment and management
- Tax planning and optimization
- Retirement planning and projections
- Integration with banking and investment systems
