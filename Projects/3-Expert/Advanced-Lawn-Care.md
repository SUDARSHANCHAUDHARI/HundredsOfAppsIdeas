# Advanced Lawn Care

## Description
An advanced lawn care platform that provides comprehensive solutions for lawn service management, scheduling, billing, and customer communication for landscaping and lawn care businesses.

## User Stories
- As a lawn care business owner, I want to manage services and customers
- As a customer, I want to schedule lawn care services
- As a technician, I want to manage my work schedule
- As an admin, I want to track business operations
- As a developer, I want to integrate lawn care features

## Bonus Features
- Service scheduling and management
- Customer relationship management
- Route optimization for technicians
- Billing and payment processing
- Weather-based scheduling
- Mobile lawn care apps
- Integration with payment processors
- Analytics and reporting

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for lawn care interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, PHP for lawn care API
- Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL for lawn care data
- Maps: Google Maps, Mapbox for route optimization
- Weather: Weather APIs for scheduling
- Payments: Stripe, PayPal for payment processing
- Analytics: Service metrics, customer insights
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for lawn care apps
