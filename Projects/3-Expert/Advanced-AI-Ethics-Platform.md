# Advanced AI Ethics Platform

## Description
A comprehensive AI ethics platform that evaluates, monitors, and ensures ethical AI deployment through bias detection, fairness analysis, and transparency reporting for machine learning systems.

## User Stories
- As an AI developer, I want to detect and mitigate bias in my models
- As a compliance officer, I want to ensure AI systems meet ethical standards
- As a researcher, I want to study AI fairness and transparency
- As a policy maker, I want to regulate AI deployment responsibly
- As a user, I want to understand AI decisions affecting me

## Bonus Features
- AI bias detection and mitigation tools
- Fairness metrics and analysis frameworks
- Explainable AI (XAI) visualization tools
- Real-time AI system monitoring
- Compliance checking for regulations (GDPR, AI Act)
- Transparency reporting and documentation
- Stakeholder impact assessment
- Integration with ML pipelines for continuous monitoring
