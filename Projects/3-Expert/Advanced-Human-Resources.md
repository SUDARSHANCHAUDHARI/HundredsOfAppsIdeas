# Advanced Human Resources

## Description
An advanced human resources platform that develops sophisticated HR solutions for talent management, employee engagement, performance analytics, and strategic workforce planning.

## User Stories
- As an HR manager, I want comprehensive employee management tools
- As a recruiter, I want talent acquisition and management
- As an employee, I want self-service HR capabilities
- As an executive, I want workforce analytics insights
- As a team lead, I want performance management tools

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered talent acquisition and matching
- Employee engagement and satisfaction analytics
- Performance management and goal tracking
- Learning and development management
- Compensation and benefits administration
- Workforce planning and analytics
- Employee self-service portals
- Integration with payroll and benefits systems
