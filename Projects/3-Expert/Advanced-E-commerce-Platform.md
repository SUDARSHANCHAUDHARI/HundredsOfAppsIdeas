# Advanced E-commerce Platform

## Description
An advanced e-commerce platform that provides comprehensive solutions for online stores, product management, payment processing, and customer experience optimization.

## User Stories
- As a store owner, I want to sell products online
- As a customer, I want to browse and purchase products easily
- As a marketer, I want to promote products and track sales
- As an admin, I want to manage inventory and orders
- As a developer, I want to customize and extend the platform

## Bonus Features
- Product catalog and inventory management
- Shopping cart and checkout process
- Payment gateway integration
- Order management and fulfillment
- Customer accounts and profiles
- Marketing and promotion tools
- Analytics and reporting
- Mobile-responsive design

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for shopping interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, PHP for e-commerce API
- Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL for store data
- Payments: Stripe, PayPal, Square integration
- Search: Elasticsearch, Algolia for product search
- Analytics: Google Analytics, custom metrics
- Integration: REST APIs for third-party services
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for shopping apps
