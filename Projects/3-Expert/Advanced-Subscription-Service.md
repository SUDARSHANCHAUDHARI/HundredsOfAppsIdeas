# Advanced Subscription Service

## Description
An advanced subscription service platform that provides comprehensive solutions for recurring billing, subscription management, and customer retention for subscription-based businesses.

## User Stories
- As a business owner, I want to manage subscription plans
- As a customer, I want to subscribe and manage my subscriptions
- As a finance manager, I want to track recurring revenue
- As a customer success manager, I want to reduce churn
- As a developer, I want to integrate subscription features

## Bonus Features
- Subscription plan management and pricing
- Recurring billing and payment processing
- Customer self-service portal
- Subscription analytics and metrics
- Dunning management and churn reduction
- Usage-based billing and metering
- Integration with accounting systems
- Mobile subscription management

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for subscription interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, PHP for subscription API
- Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL for subscription data
- Payments: Stripe, PayPal for recurring billing
- Analytics: Revenue tracking, churn analysis
- Integration: REST APIs for third-party services
- Security: PCI compliance, data encryption
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for subscription apps
