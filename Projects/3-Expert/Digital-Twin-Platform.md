# Digital Twin Platform

## Description
An advanced digital twin platform that creates real-time, high-fidelity virtual replicas of physical systems, processes, and environments for simulation, monitoring, optimization, and predictive maintenance.

## User Stories
- As an engineer, I want to create digital twins of physical systems
- As a manufacturer, I want to monitor and optimize production processes
- As a city planner, I want to simulate urban systems and infrastructure
- As a healthcare provider, I want to create patient digital twins
- As a researcher, I want to study complex systems through digital twins

## Bonus Features
- Real-time synchronization with physical systems
- High-fidelity physics simulation
- Predictive maintenance and failure prediction
- Multi-scale digital twin modeling
- AI-powered optimization and control
- Augmented reality visualization
- Digital twin lifecycle management
- Integration with IoT and sensor networks

- Simulation: Physics engines, real-time simulation
