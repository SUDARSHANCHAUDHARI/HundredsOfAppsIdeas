# Genomic Medicine Platform

## Description
An advanced genomic medicine platform that uses AI to analyze genetic data, personalize medical treatments, and accelerate drug discovery based on individual genetic profiles.

## User Stories
- As a geneticist, I want to analyze and interpret genomic data
- As a doctor, I want to prescribe personalized treatments based on genetics
- As a pharmaceutical researcher, I want to develop targeted therapies
- As a patient, I want to understand my genetic health risks
- As a public health official, I want to track genetic disease patterns

## Bonus Features
- Whole genome sequencing and analysis
- AI-powered genetic variant interpretation
- Personalized drug response prediction
- Genetic disease risk assessment
- Pharmacogenomics treatment optimization
- Gene editing and CRISPR integration
- Population genetics and epidemiology
- Clinical trial matching based on genetics
