# Advanced Patent Management

## Description
An advanced patent management platform that develops sophisticated solutions for patent tracking, portfolio management, innovation analysis, and comprehensive intellectual property strategy development.

## User Stories
- As a patent manager, I want comprehensive patent tools
- As an inventor, I want patent application assistance
- As a legal professional, I want patent portfolio management
- As a business owner, I want IP strategy optimization
- As an analyst, I want patent analytics

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered patent search and analysis
- Automated patent application and filing
- Patent portfolio optimization and management
- Innovation trend analysis and insights
- Multi-jurisdiction patent protection
- Integration with legal and IP systems
- Patent valuation and licensing
- Compliance and regulatory support
