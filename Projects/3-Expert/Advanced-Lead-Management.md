# Advanced Lead Management

## Description
An advanced lead management platform that provides comprehensive solutions for capturing, nurturing, and converting sales leads with automated workflows and analytics.

## User Stories
- As a sales rep, I want to track and manage my leads
- As a marketer, I want to capture and qualify leads
- As a sales manager, I want to monitor lead conversion
- As an admin, I want to manage lead data and workflows
- As a developer, I want to integrate lead features

## Bonus Features
- Lead capture and import tools
- Lead scoring and qualification
- Automated lead nurturing workflows
- Lead assignment and routing
- Sales pipeline management
- Lead analytics and reporting
- Integration with marketing tools
- Mobile lead management

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React, Vue.js for lead interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, PHP for lead API
- Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL for lead data
- Automation: Workflow engines, lead nurturing
- Analytics: Lead conversion, pipeline metrics
- Integration: Marketing automation, CRM systems
- Security: Lead data protection, compliance
- Mobile: React Native, Flutter for lead apps
