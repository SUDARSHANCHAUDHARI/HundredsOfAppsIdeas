# Smart Disaster Recovery

## Description
An intelligent disaster recovery platform that uses AI to optimize recovery operations, track recovery readiness, and provide personalized recovery insights through smart disaster recovery tools.

## User Stories
- As a disaster recovery enthusiast, I want AI-powered recovery optimization
- As a disaster recovery specialist, I want to manage recovery operations efficiently
- As a business continuity manager, I want to ensure business resilience
- As a system administrator, I want to ensure rapid recovery from disasters
- As a business owner, I want to protect my business from disruptions

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered disaster recovery optimization and insights
- Integration with disaster recovery tools and business continuity systems
- Personalized disaster recovery experience tracking and analytics
- Social features for disaster recovery communities
- Multiple disaster recovery domains and optimization strategies
- Integration with backup and monitoring systems
- Disaster recovery creation tools for organizations
- Integration with recovery testing and validation
