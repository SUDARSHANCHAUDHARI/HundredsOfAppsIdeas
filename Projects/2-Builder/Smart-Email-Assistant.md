# Smart Email Assistant

## Description
An AI-powered email management system that automatically categorizes emails, suggests responses, and optimizes communication patterns for enhanced productivity and inbox organization.

## User Stories
- As a professional, I want AI-powered email categorization and prioritization
- As a busy executive, I want intelligent email response suggestions
- As a marketer, I want to optimize email campaigns and engagement
- As a customer support agent, I want automated email routing and responses
- As a user, I want to reduce email overload and improve productivity

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered email categorization and priority scoring
- Intelligent response suggestions and templates
- Email sentiment analysis and tone detection
- Automated follow-up reminders and scheduling
- Integration with calendar and task management
- Email analytics and communication insights
- Spam and phishing detection with AI
- Multi-language support and translation

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with email management interface
- Backend: Python with NLP and machine learning
- AI: TensorFlow for email analysis and prediction
- Database: MongoDB with email metadata and patterns
- APIs: Gmail, Outlook, and email service APIs
- NLP: Natural language processing for text analysis
- Real-time: WebSocket for live email updates
- Security: End-to-end encryption for email privacy
