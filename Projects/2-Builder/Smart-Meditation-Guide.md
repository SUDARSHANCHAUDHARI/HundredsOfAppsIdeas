# Smart Meditation Guide

## Description
An intelligent meditation and mindfulness platform that uses AI to personalize meditation sessions, track progress, and provide adaptive guidance based on user stress levels and goals.

## User Stories
- As a meditation practitioner, I want personalized meditation recommendations
- As a stressed professional, I want stress relief and relaxation guidance
- As a beginner, I want guided meditation and learning resources
- As a health enthusiast, I want to track meditation progress and benefits
- As a therapist, I want to recommend meditation to clients effectively

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered meditation personalization and adaptation
- Integration with wearable devices for stress monitoring
- Biometric feedback integration (heart rate, breathing)
- Sleep quality improvement through meditation
- Integration with mental health and wellness apps
- Social meditation and community features
- Meditation progress tracking and analytics
- Integration with smart home for ambient environments
