# Smart Risk Management

## Description
An intelligent risk management platform that uses AI to identify and mitigate risks, track risk exposure, and provide personalized risk assessment experiences through smart risk management tools.

## User Stories
- As a risk management enthusiast, I want AI-powered risk identification
- As a risk manager, I want to assess and mitigate risks effectively
- As a project manager, I want to manage project risks proactively
- As a business owner, I want to protect my business from risks
- As an investor, I want to optimize risk-reward strategies

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered risk management optimization and insights
- Integration with risk assessment and compliance tools
- Personalized risk management experience tracking and analytics
- Social features for risk management communities
- Multiple risk domains and mitigation strategies
- Integration with insurance and legal compliance tools
- Risk management creation tools for organizations
- Integration with predictive analytics and scenario planning
