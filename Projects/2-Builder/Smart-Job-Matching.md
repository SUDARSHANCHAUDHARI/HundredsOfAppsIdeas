# Smart Job Matching

## Description
An intelligent job matching platform that uses AI to connect job seekers with ideal opportunities, track application progress, and provide personalized career recommendations through smart job search tools.

## User Stories
- As a job matching enthusiast, I want AI-powered job recommendations
- As a job seeker, I want to find the perfect job for my skills and preferences
- As an employer, I want to find and attract qualified candidates
- As a recruiter, I want to streamline the hiring process
- As a career counselor, I want to support job seekers effectively

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered job matching and compatibility analysis
- Integration with skill assessment and career development
- Personalized job search tracking and analytics
- Social features for job seeking communities
- Multiple job domains and opportunity types
- Integration with job boards and professional networks
- Job matching creation tools for employers and recruiters
- Integration with resume optimization and interview preparation

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with job matching interface
- Backend: Python with AI and job processing
- AI: TensorFlow for job matching and recommendations
- Database: MongoDB with job data and candidate profiles
- Job Engine: Custom matching and application tracking
- Analytics: Job search effectiveness and matching accuracy
- Integration: Job boards and professional network APIs
- Social: Community features and job search sharing
