# Smart Podcast Producer

## Description
An intelligent podcast production platform that automates audio editing, generates show notes, optimizes content for engagement, and provides AI-powered insights for successful podcast creation.

## User Stories
- As a podcaster, I want AI assistance for audio editing and production
- As a content creator, I want to generate show notes and transcripts automatically
- As a marketer, I want to optimize podcast content for audience engagement
- As a producer, I want to manage multiple podcasts efficiently
- As a listener, I want to discover and engage with relevant podcast content

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered audio editing and noise reduction
- Automated transcription and show note generation
- Content optimization for search and discovery
- Audience analytics and engagement tracking
- Integration with podcast distribution platforms
- Automated episode scheduling and publishing
- Content recommendation and topic suggestions
- Monetization optimization and sponsorship matching
