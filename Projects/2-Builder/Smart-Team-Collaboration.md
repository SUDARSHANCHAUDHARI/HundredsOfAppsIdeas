# Smart Team Collaboration Platform

## Description
An intelligent team collaboration platform that uses AI to optimize team communication, automate workflows, and enhance productivity through smart meeting management and project coordination.

## User Stories
- As a team leader, I want AI-powered meeting scheduling and optimization
- As a team member, I want intelligent task assignment and progress tracking
- As a remote worker, I want to maintain team cohesion and communication
- As a project manager, I want to optimize team productivity and workflows
- As an HR manager, I want to analyze team dynamics and performance

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered meeting scheduling and optimization
- Intelligent task assignment based on team member skills
- Team communication analysis and productivity insights
- Automated workflow optimization and suggestions
- Integration with popular collaboration tools
- Team sentiment analysis and well-being monitoring
- Smart document collaboration and version control
- Virtual team building and engagement activities

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with collaboration interfaces
- Backend: Node.js with AI and team analytics
- AI: TensorFlow for team optimization and analysis
- Database: MongoDB with team and project data
- APIs: Collaboration, video conferencing, and project management APIs
- Real-time: WebSocket for live collaboration
- Analytics: Team productivity and communication analysis
- Integration: Multiple collaboration platform APIs
