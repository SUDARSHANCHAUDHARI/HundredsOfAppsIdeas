# Personal Finance AI Advisor

## Description
An AI-powered personal finance advisor that provides intelligent financial advice, tracks spending patterns, and helps users achieve their financial goals through machine learning insights.

## User Stories
- As a user, I want AI to analyze my spending patterns and suggest optimizations
- As a user, I want personalized investment recommendations based on my risk profile
- As a user, I want AI to help me create and track financial goals
- As a user, I want real-time fraud detection and alerts
- As a user, I want AI to predict future expenses and help with budgeting

## Bonus Features
- Integration with bank accounts and credit cards
- AI-powered tax optimization suggestions
- Retirement planning with Monte Carlo simulations
- Debt payoff strategy recommendations
- Subscription management and cancellation suggestions
- Bill negotiation assistance
- Credit score improvement recommendations
- Cryptocurrency portfolio tracking and analysis
