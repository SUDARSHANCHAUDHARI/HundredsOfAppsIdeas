# Smart Business Continuity

## Description
An intelligent business continuity platform that uses AI to optimize continuity planning, track continuity readiness, and provide personalized continuity insights through smart business continuity tools.

## User Stories
- As a business continuity enthusiast, I want AI-powered continuity optimization
- As a business continuity manager, I want to manage continuity plans effectively
- As a risk manager, I want to ensure business resilience to disruptions
- As an executive, I want to protect business operations from interruptions
- As a stakeholder, I want to ensure business sustainability and growth

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered business continuity optimization and insights
- Integration with business continuity tools and risk management systems
- Personalized business continuity experience tracking and analytics
- Social features for business continuity communities
- Multiple business continuity domains and optimization strategies
- Integration with disaster recovery and emergency response systems
- Business continuity creation tools for organizations
- Integration with crisis management and communication
