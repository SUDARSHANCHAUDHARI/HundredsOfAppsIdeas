# Digital Art Gallery

## Description
A virtual art gallery platform that showcases digital artwork with immersive 3D environments, AI-powered art recommendations, and blockchain-based art authentication.

## User Stories
- As an artist, I want to showcase and sell my digital artwork
- As an art collector, I want to discover and purchase unique digital art
- As a gallery owner, I want to curate exhibitions and manage collections
- As an art enthusiast, I want to explore art in immersive virtual spaces
- As an investor, I want to track art value and authenticity

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered art style analysis and recommendations
- Virtual reality (VR) gallery tours
- Blockchain-based art provenance and authentication
- Augmented reality art placement in physical spaces
- Interactive art installations and experiences
- Artist collaboration tools and workshops
- Art market analytics and price tracking
- Social features for art community engagement

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with Three.js for 3D galleries
- Backend: Node.js with blockchain integration
- Database: MongoDB with art metadata
- Blockchain: Ethereum/NFT for art authentication
- VR: WebXR API for virtual reality
- AI: TensorFlow for art style analysis
- Storage: IPFS for decentralized art storage
- Payment: Cryptocurrency and traditional payment methods
