# Smart Food Recommendation System

## Description
An intelligent food recommendation platform that uses AI to suggest personalized recipes, restaurants, and meal plans based on dietary preferences, health goals, and taste preferences.

## User Stories
- As a foodie, I want personalized restaurant and recipe recommendations
- As a health-conscious person, I want meal suggestions based on dietary needs
- As a home cook, I want recipe suggestions based on available ingredients
- As a traveler, I want to discover local cuisine and restaurants
- As a nutritionist, I want to track and optimize client meal plans

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered taste preference learning and adaptation
- Integration with food delivery and restaurant APIs
- Nutritional analysis and health optimization
- Integration with grocery shopping and meal planning
- Social features for sharing food experiences
- Integration with fitness and health tracking apps
- Food allergy and dietary restriction management
- Integration with smart kitchen appliances

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with food discovery interface
- Backend: Python with recommendation algorithms
- AI: TensorFlow for taste preference analysis
- Database: MongoDB with user preferences and food data
- APIs: Yelp, Edamam, and food delivery APIs
- NLP: Natural language processing for recipe analysis
- Integration: Fitness and health tracking APIs
- Mobile: React Native for mobile food discovery
