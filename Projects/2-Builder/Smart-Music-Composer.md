# Smart Music Composer

## Description
An AI-powered music composition platform that creates original music, suggests harmonies, and helps musicians compose and arrange songs through intelligent music generation and analysis.

## User Stories
- As a musician, I want AI assistance for composing original music
- As a producer, I want to generate backing tracks and arrangements
- As a student, I want to learn music theory through composition
- As a content creator, I want to generate royalty-free background music
- As a music enthusiast, I want to create personalized music playlists

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered melody and harmony generation
- Integration with digital audio workstations (DAWs)
- Music style analysis and genre blending
- Real-time music generation and improvisation
- Integration with music theory education
- Collaborative composition and sharing
- Music analysis and feedback system
- Integration with streaming platforms and music libraries

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with music composition interface
- Backend: Python with music generation libraries
- AI: TensorFlow/Magenta for music generation
- Database: MongoDB with music compositions and patterns
- APIs: Spotify, Apple Music, and music streaming APIs
- Audio: Real-time audio processing and synthesis
- Machine Learning: Music style and pattern analysis
- Integration: DAW and music production APIs
