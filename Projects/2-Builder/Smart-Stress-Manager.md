# Smart Stress Manager

## Description
An intelligent stress management platform that uses AI to monitor stress levels, provide personalized relaxation techniques, and integrate with wellness apps for comprehensive stress reduction.

## User Stories
- As a user, I want AI-powered stress level monitoring
- As a busy professional, I want effective stress management techniques
- As a student, I want to manage academic stress effectively
- As a wellness enthusiast, I want to improve mental well-being
- As a therapist, I want to track client stress levels

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered stress detection and prediction
- Integration with wearable devices for biometric monitoring
- Personalized relaxation and meditation recommendations
- Integration with wellness and mental health apps
- Real-time stress alerts and intervention suggestions
- Integration with workplace wellness programs
- Social features for stress management support
- Integration with healthcare professionals
