# Smart Workplace Wellness

## Description
An intelligent workplace wellness platform that uses AI to optimize employee health, track wellness programs, and provide personalized wellness experiences through smart workplace wellness tools.

## User Stories
- As a workplace wellness enthusiast, I want AI-powered wellness optimization
- As an HR professional, I want to improve employee health effectively
- As an employee, I want to maintain wellness at work
- As a manager, I want to support team wellness and health
- As an executive, I want to optimize organizational wellness strategy

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered workplace wellness optimization and insights
- Integration with health tracking and wellness programs
- Personalized wellness experience tracking and analytics
- Social features for workplace wellness communities
- Multiple wellness domains and improvement strategies
- Integration with HR systems and employee benefits
- Workplace wellness creation tools for organizations
- Integration with mental health and stress management

- Frontend: React with workplace wellness interface

- Database: MongoDB with wellness data and programs

- Analytics: Wellness trends and health outcome tracking
