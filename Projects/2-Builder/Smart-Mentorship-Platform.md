# Smart Mentorship Platform

## Description
An intelligent mentorship platform that uses AI to match mentors and mentees, track relationship development, and provide personalized guidance through smart mentorship tools and resources.

## User Stories
- As a mentorship enthusiast, I want AI-powered mentorship matching
- As a mentee, I want to find the perfect mentor for my goals
- As a mentor, I want to effectively guide and support my mentees
- As an organization, I want to manage mentorship programs efficiently
- As a professional, I want to both give and receive mentorship

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered mentorship matching and compatibility analysis
- Integration with professional development and career guidance
- Personalized mentorship relationship tracking and analytics
- Social features for mentorship communities
- Multiple mentorship domains and relationship types
- Integration with professional networks and educational platforms
- Mentorship program creation tools for organizations
- Integration with skill development and goal tracking

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with mentorship platform interface
- Backend: Python with AI and mentorship processing
- AI: TensorFlow for mentorship matching and guidance
- Database: MongoDB with mentorship data and relationships
- Mentorship Engine: Custom matching and relationship management
- Analytics: Mentorship effectiveness and progress tracking
- Integration: Professional networks and career APIs
- Social: Community features and mentorship sharing
