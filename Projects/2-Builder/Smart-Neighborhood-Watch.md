# Smart Neighborhood Watch

## Description
An intelligent neighborhood watch platform that uses AI to optimize community monitoring, track suspicious activities, and provide personalized neighborhood safety insights through smart watch tools.

## User Stories
- As a neighborhood watch enthusiast, I want AI-powered community monitoring
- As a neighborhood watch coordinator, I want to manage watch activities effectively
- As a community volunteer, I want to participate in neighborhood safety
- As a resident, I want to contribute to community safety efforts
- As a local business owner, I want to support neighborhood safety initiatives

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered neighborhood watch optimization and insights
- Integration with neighborhood watch tools and community monitoring systems
- Personalized neighborhood watch experience tracking and analytics
- Social features for neighborhood watch communities
- Multiple neighborhood watch domains and monitoring strategies
- Integration with local law enforcement and emergency services
- Neighborhood watch creation tools for communities
- Integration with suspicious activity reporting and analysis

- Frontend: React with neighborhood watch interface
