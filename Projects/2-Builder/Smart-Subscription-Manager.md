# Smart Subscription Manager

## Description
An intelligent subscription management platform that tracks all recurring payments, identifies unused subscriptions, and provides cost optimization recommendations through AI-powered analysis.

## User Stories
- As a consumer, I want to track all my subscriptions in one place
- As a budget-conscious user, I want to identify and cancel unused subscriptions
- As a family manager, I want to coordinate family subscriptions efficiently
- As a business owner, I want to manage software and service subscriptions
- As a financial advisor, I want to help clients optimize subscription costs

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered subscription usage analysis and recommendations
- Integration with bank accounts and payment processors
- Automatic subscription detection and categorization
- Cost optimization and alternative service suggestions
- Family subscription sharing and coordination
- Subscription price tracking and alerts
- Integration with budgeting and financial apps
- Subscription cancellation assistance and negotiation
