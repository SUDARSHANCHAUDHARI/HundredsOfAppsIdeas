# Smart Career Coach

## Description
An AI-powered career development platform that provides personalized career guidance, skill gap analysis, and job market insights to help users advance their professional careers.

## User Stories
- As a job seeker, I want AI-powered career path recommendations
- As a professional, I want to identify skill gaps and learning opportunities
- As a career changer, I want to explore new career options and requirements
- As a student, I want to align education with career goals
- As a mentor, I want to track mentee progress and provide guidance

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered career path optimization
- Integration with job boards and recruitment platforms
- Skill assessment and gap analysis
- Resume optimization and interview preparation
- Salary negotiation guidance and market analysis
- Professional network building and mentorship matching
- Industry trend analysis and future-proofing
- Integration with learning platforms and certification programs
