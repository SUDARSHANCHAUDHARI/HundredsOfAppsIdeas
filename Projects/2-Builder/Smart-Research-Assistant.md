# Smart Research Assistant

## Description
An AI-powered research assistant that helps users gather, analyze, and synthesize information from multiple sources with automated literature reviews, citation management, and research insights.

## User Stories
- As a researcher, I want AI-powered literature review and synthesis
- As a student, I want help with research paper writing and citations
- As an academic, I want to track research trends and find collaborators
- As a journalist, I want to fact-check and verify information quickly
- As a business analyst, I want to gather market intelligence efficiently

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered literature review and summarization
- Automated citation management and formatting
- Research trend analysis and prediction
- Fact-checking and source verification
- Collaborative research tools and sharing
- Integration with academic databases and journals
- Research methodology recommendations
- Plagiarism detection and originality checking

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with research management interface
- Backend: Python with AI and academic APIs
- AI: TensorFlow for research analysis and synthesis
- Database: MongoDB with research data and citations
- APIs: Academic databases and search APIs
- NLP: Natural language processing for text analysis
- Integration: Academic database and journal APIs
- Security: Secure handling of research data
