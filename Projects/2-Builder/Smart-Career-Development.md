# Smart Career Development

## Description
An intelligent career development platform that uses AI to provide personalized career guidance, track professional growth, and offer adaptive skill development through engaging career planning tools.

## User Stories
- As a career development enthusiast, I want AI-powered career guidance
- As a job seeker, I want personalized career path recommendations
- As a professional, I want to track and plan my career growth
- As a student, I want to explore career options and required skills
- As an HR professional, I want to support employee career development

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered career development planning and guidance
- Integration with labor market data and industry trends
- Personalized career path tracking and analytics
- Social features for career development communities
- Multiple career domains and development paths
- Integration with job platforms and professional networks
- Career development creation tools for counselors and HR
- Integration with mentorship and coaching programs

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with career development interface
- Backend: Python with AI and career processing
- AI: TensorFlow for career development guidance
- Database: MongoDB with career data and development paths
- Career Engine: Custom career planning and adaptation
- Analytics: Career growth and skill development tracking
- Integration: Job platforms and professional network APIs
- Social: Community features and career development sharing
