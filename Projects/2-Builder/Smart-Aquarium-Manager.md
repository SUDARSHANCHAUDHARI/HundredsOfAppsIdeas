# Smart Aquarium Manager

## Description
An intelligent aquarium management system that uses IoT sensors and AI to monitor water quality, automate feeding, and maintain optimal conditions for aquatic life.

## User Stories
- As an aquarium owner, I want automated water quality monitoring
- As a fish enthusiast, I want optimal conditions for my aquatic life
- As a busy person, I want automated feeding and maintenance
- As a researcher, I want to study aquatic ecosystems
- As a pet store owner, I want to manage multiple aquariums efficiently

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered water quality prediction and optimization
- Integration with IoT sensors for real-time monitoring
- Automated feeding and maintenance schedules
- Integration with smart home systems
- Aquatic life health monitoring and alerts
- Integration with aquatic research databases
- Social features for sharing aquarium setups
- Integration with aquatic supply ordering

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with aquarium monitoring interface
- Backend: Python with AI and IoT integration
- AI: TensorFlow for water quality prediction
- Database: MongoDB with aquarium data and sensor readings
- APIs: IoT sensor and aquarium APIs
- IoT: Integration with water quality sensors
- Real-time: WebSocket for live monitoring
- Integration: Smart home and aquatic supply APIs
