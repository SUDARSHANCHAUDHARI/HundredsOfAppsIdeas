# Smart Community Safety

## Description
An intelligent community safety platform that uses AI to optimize neighborhood safety, track safety incidents, and provide personalized community safety insights through smart community safety tools.

## User Stories
- As a community safety enthusiast, I want AI-powered neighborhood safety optimization
- As a community leader, I want to manage neighborhood safety effectively
- As a neighborhood watch member, I want to coordinate safety efforts
- As a resident, I want to feel safe and informed about community safety
- As a local business owner, I want to ensure business and customer safety

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered community safety optimization and insights
- Integration with community safety tools and neighborhood watch systems
- Personalized community safety experience tracking and analytics
- Social features for community safety communities
- Multiple community safety domains and protection strategies
- Integration with local law enforcement and emergency services
- Community safety creation tools for neighborhoods
- Integration with crime reporting and prevention
