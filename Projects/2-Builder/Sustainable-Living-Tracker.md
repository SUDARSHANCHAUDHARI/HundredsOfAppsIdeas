# Sustainable Living Tracker

## Description
A comprehensive sustainability tracking app that helps users monitor their environmental impact, suggest eco-friendly alternatives, and gamify sustainable living habits.

## User Stories
- As a user, I want to track my carbon footprint from daily activities
- As a user, I want AI-powered suggestions for reducing environmental impact
- As a user, I want to find eco-friendly products and services
- As a user, I want to join sustainability challenges with friends
- As a user, I want to earn rewards for sustainable actions

## Bonus Features
- Integration with smart devices for automatic tracking
- Local recycling and composting facility finder
- Sustainable transportation route planning
- Energy usage optimization for home
- Water conservation tracking and tips
- Sustainable shopping recommendations
- Community challenges and leaderboards
- Integration with carbon offset programs

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with interactive charts and maps
- Backend: Node.js with environmental data APIs
- Database: MongoDB with geospatial queries
- APIs: EPA environmental data, carbon footprint calculators
- Maps: Google Maps or Mapbox for location features
- Gamification: Badge system and achievement tracking
