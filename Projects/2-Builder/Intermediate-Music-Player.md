# Intermediate Music Player

## Description
An intermediate music player application that allows users to play, organize, and manage their music library with playlists, equalizer, and music discovery features.

## User Stories
- As a music lover, I want to play and organize my music
- As a user, I want to create and manage playlists
- As a music enthusiast, I want to discover new music
- As a developer, I want to build a music application

## Bonus Features
- Music playback controls
- Playlist creation and management
- Music library organization
- Equalizer and audio controls
- Music search and filtering
- Album artwork and metadata
- Shuffle and repeat modes
- Music recommendations
