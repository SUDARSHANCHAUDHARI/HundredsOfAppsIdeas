# Smart Identity Management

## Description
An intelligent identity management platform that uses AI to optimize identity operations, track identity patterns, and provide personalized identity insights through smart identity management tools.

## User Stories
- As an identity management enthusiast, I want AI-powered identity optimization
- As an identity manager, I want to manage user identities effectively
- As a security professional, I want to ensure secure identity management
- As a business owner, I want to protect user identities and data
- As a user, I want to manage my digital identity securely

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered identity management optimization and insights
- Integration with identity management tools and security frameworks
- Personalized identity management experience tracking and analytics
- Social features for identity management communities
- Multiple identity management domains and security strategies
- Integration with authentication systems and access control
- Identity management creation tools for organizations
- Integration with compliance and audit systems
