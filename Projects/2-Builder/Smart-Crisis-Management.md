# Smart Crisis Management

## Description
An intelligent crisis management platform that uses AI to optimize crisis response, track crisis situations, and provide personalized crisis insights through smart crisis management tools.

## User Stories
- As a crisis management enthusiast, I want AI-powered crisis optimization
- As a crisis manager, I want to manage crisis situations effectively
- As an emergency responder, I want to coordinate response operations
- As a communications specialist, I want to manage crisis communications
- As an executive, I want to protect stakeholders during crises

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered crisis management optimization and insights
- Integration with crisis management tools and emergency response systems
- Personalized crisis management experience tracking and analytics
- Social features for crisis management communities
- Multiple crisis domains and response strategies
- Integration with communication and monitoring systems
- Crisis management creation tools for organizations
- Integration with incident reporting and analysis

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with crisis management interface
- Backend: Python with AI and crisis processing
- AI: TensorFlow for crisis management optimization
- Database: MongoDB with crisis metadata and response data
- Crisis Engine: Custom crisis management automation and monitoring
- Analytics: Crisis response and effectiveness tracking
- Integration: Crisis management tools and communication APIs
- Social: Community features and crisis management sharing
