# Smart Art Gallery

## Description
An intelligent art gallery platform that uses AI to curate exhibitions, provide art analysis, and create immersive virtual gallery experiences with personalized art recommendations.

## User Stories
- As an art enthusiast, I want personalized art recommendations and insights
- As a gallery owner, I want to create virtual exhibitions and manage collections
- As an artist, I want to showcase and sell my artwork digitally
- As a student, I want to learn about art history and techniques
- As a collector, I want to discover and acquire art intelligently

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered art analysis and style identification
- Virtual reality (VR) gallery tours and exhibitions
- Personalized art recommendations based on preferences
- Integration with art marketplaces and auction houses
- Art authentication and provenance tracking
- Interactive art education and tutorials
- Social features for art community engagement
- Integration with augmented reality for art visualization

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with art gallery interface
- Backend: Python with AI and art analysis
- AI: TensorFlow for art style and pattern recognition
- Database: MongoDB with art collections and metadata
- APIs: Artsy, museum, and art marketplace APIs
- VR: WebXR for virtual gallery experiences
- Computer Vision: Art analysis and identification
- Integration: Art marketplace and museum APIs
