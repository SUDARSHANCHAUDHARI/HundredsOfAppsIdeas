# Smart Customer Service Platform

## Description
An AI-powered customer service platform that combines chatbots, sentiment analysis, and intelligent routing to provide exceptional customer support across multiple channels.

## User Stories
- As a customer service manager, I want AI-powered chatbot automation
- As a support agent, I want intelligent ticket routing and suggestions
- As a customer, I want 24/7 support across multiple channels
- As a business owner, I want to analyze customer satisfaction trends
- As a quality analyst, I want to monitor and improve service quality

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered sentiment analysis and emotion detection
- Multi-channel support (chat, email, social media, phone)
- Intelligent ticket routing and prioritization
- Real-time agent assistance and knowledge base suggestions
- Customer satisfaction prediction and analytics
- Integration with CRM and helpdesk systems
- Voice recognition and speech-to-text for phone support
- Automated quality assurance and performance monitoring

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with customer service dashboards
- Backend: Node.js with AI and NLP capabilities
- AI: TensorFlow for sentiment analysis and prediction
- Database: MongoDB with customer interaction data
- APIs: CRM, helpdesk, and communication APIs
- NLP: Natural language processing for chatbots
- Real-time: WebSocket for live customer interactions
- Integration: Multi-channel communication platforms
