# Smart Calendar Assistant

## Description
An AI-powered calendar assistant that intelligently schedules meetings, optimizes time management, and provides contextual suggestions for productivity enhancement.

## User Stories
- As a professional, I want AI to schedule meetings based on availability
- As a team member, I want intelligent meeting time suggestions
- As a busy person, I want automated calendar optimization
- As a remote worker, I want time zone management for global teams
- As a student, I want study schedule optimization

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered meeting preparation and follow-up automation
- Integration with email for automatic event creation
- Travel time calculation and buffer scheduling
- Energy level tracking and optimal task timing
- Integration with project management tools
- Recurring pattern optimization
- Meeting effectiveness analytics
- Integration with focus time and deep work scheduling
