# Smart Plant Care System

## Description
An intelligent plant care platform that uses AI to monitor plant health, provide personalized care recommendations, and automate watering and maintenance for optimal plant growth.

## User Stories
- As a plant owner, I want AI-powered plant health monitoring
- As a gardener, I want personalized care recommendations
- As a busy person, I want automated plant maintenance
- As a botanist, I want to study plant growth patterns
- As a nursery owner, I want to manage multiple plants efficiently

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered plant disease detection and treatment
- Integration with IoT sensors for real-time monitoring
- Automated watering and care schedules
- Integration with plant identification databases
- Plant growth tracking and optimization
- Integration with smart home and garden systems
- Social features for sharing plant care tips
- Integration with plant supply ordering
