# Smart Internship Platform

## Description
An intelligent internship platform that uses AI to match students with opportunities, track internship progress, and provide personalized career development through smart internship management tools.

## User Stories
- As an internship platform enthusiast, I want AI-powered internship matching
- As a student, I want to find the perfect internship for my career goals
- As an employer, I want to find and manage interns effectively
- As an educator, I want to support student internship experiences
- As a career counselor, I want to track student internship outcomes

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered internship matching and compatibility analysis
- Integration with career development and skill assessment
- Personalized internship progress tracking and analytics
- Social features for internship communities
- Multiple internship domains and opportunity types
- Integration with educational institutions and employers
- Internship program creation tools for organizations
- Integration with skill development and career planning

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with internship platform interface
- Backend: Python with AI and internship processing
- AI: TensorFlow for internship matching and management
- Database: MongoDB with internship data and opportunities
- Internship Engine: Custom matching and progress tracking
- Analytics: Internship effectiveness and skill development tracking
- Integration: Educational institutions and employer APIs
- Social: Community features and internship sharing
