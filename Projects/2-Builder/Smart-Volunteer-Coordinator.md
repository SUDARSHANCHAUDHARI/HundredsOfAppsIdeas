# Smart Volunteer Coordinator

## Description
An intelligent volunteer management platform that matches volunteers with opportunities, tracks impact, and optimizes community engagement through AI-powered recommendations and analytics.

## User Stories
- As a volunteer, I want personalized volunteer opportunities based on my skills
- As an organization, I want to efficiently manage and coordinate volunteers
- As a community leader, I want to track volunteer impact and engagement
- As a nonprofit, I want to recruit and retain volunteers effectively
- As a corporate sponsor, I want to track employee volunteer programs

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered volunteer-opportunity matching
- Integration with nonprofit and community databases
- Volunteer impact tracking and analytics
- Skills assessment and development recommendations
- Integration with corporate social responsibility programs
- Volunteer scheduling and coordination tools
- Community needs assessment and gap analysis
- Social impact reporting and visualization

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with volunteer management interface
- Backend: Node.js with volunteer coordination logic
- AI: TensorFlow for volunteer matching and optimization
- Database: MongoDB with volunteer and opportunity data
- APIs: Volunteer and nonprofit databases
- Analytics: Impact tracking and visualization
- Mobile: React Native for on-the-go volunteering
- Integration: Corporate social responsibility APIs
