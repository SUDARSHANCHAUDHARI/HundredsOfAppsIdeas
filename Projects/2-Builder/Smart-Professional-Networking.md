# Smart Professional Networking

## Description
An intelligent professional networking platform that uses AI to optimize networking opportunities, track relationship development, and provide personalized career connections through smart networking tools.

## User Stories
- As a professional networking enthusiast, I want AI-powered networking assistance
- As a job seeker, I want to find relevant professional connections
- As a professional, I want to build and maintain my network effectively
- As a recruiter, I want to find and connect with qualified candidates
- As a business owner, I want to expand my professional network

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered professional networking optimization and recommendations
- Integration with professional networks and industry data
- Personalized networking strategy tracking and analytics
- Social features for professional networking communities
- Multiple networking domains and connection types
- Integration with career development and job platforms
- Professional networking creation tools for users and organizations
- Integration with mentorship and collaboration programs
