# Smart Content Creator

## Description
An AI-powered content creation platform that generates, optimizes, and distributes content across multiple channels with personalized recommendations and performance analytics.

## User Stories
- As a content creator, I want AI-powered content generation and optimization
- As a marketer, I want to create personalized content for different audiences
- As a social media manager, I want to optimize content for each platform
- As a blogger, I want to generate ideas and improve writing quality
- As a business owner, I want to create engaging content efficiently

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered content generation for multiple formats
- Content optimization for different platforms and audiences
- Performance analytics and engagement tracking
- SEO optimization and keyword suggestions
- Multi-language content creation and translation
- Content calendar and scheduling automation
- Brand voice consistency across all content
- Integration with social media and publishing platforms

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with content creation and editing interfaces
- Backend: Python with AI and NLP capabilities
- AI: TensorFlow/OpenAI for content generation
- Database: MongoDB with content and performance data
- APIs: Social media, SEO, and publishing APIs
- NLP: Natural language processing for content optimization
- Analytics: Content performance and engagement tracking
- Integration: Multi-platform publishing tools
