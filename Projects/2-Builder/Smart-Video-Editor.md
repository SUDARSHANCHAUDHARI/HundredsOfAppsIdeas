# Smart Video Editor

## Description
An AI-powered video editing platform that automates video production, suggests edits, and optimizes content for different platforms with intelligent scene detection and enhancement features.

## User Stories
- As a content creator, I want AI assistance for video editing and production
- As a social media manager, I want to optimize videos for different platforms
- As a marketer, I want to create engaging video content efficiently
- As a filmmaker, I want to streamline the editing process
- As a student, I want to learn video editing through AI guidance

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered scene detection and automatic editing
- Integration with stock video and music libraries
- Platform-specific video optimization
- Automated caption generation and translation
- Video quality enhancement and stabilization
- Integration with social media platforms
- Real-time collaboration and feedback
- Video analytics and performance tracking

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with video editing interface
- Backend: Python with video processing and AI
- AI: TensorFlow for video analysis and editing
- Database: MongoDB with video projects and metadata
- APIs: YouTube, Vimeo, and social media APIs
- Video Processing: FFmpeg for video manipulation
- Computer Vision: OpenCV for scene detection
- Integration: Social media and video platform APIs
