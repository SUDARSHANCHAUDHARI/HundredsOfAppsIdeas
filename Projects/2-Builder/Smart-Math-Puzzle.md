# Smart Math Puzzle

## Description
An intelligent math puzzle platform that uses AI to create adaptive mathematical challenges, track problem-solving development, and provide personalized learning through engaging math puzzles.

## User Stories
- As a math puzzle enthusiast, I want AI-powered mathematical challenges
- As a student, I want to improve problem-solving through math puzzles
- As a teacher, I want educational math puzzles for students
- As a mathematician, I want to enhance mathematical thinking
- As a puzzle lover, I want to challenge my mathematical reasoning

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered math puzzle generation and adaptation
- Integration with mathematical reasoning and problem-solving
- Personalized mathematical problem-solving development tracking
- Social features for math puzzle communities
- Multiple math puzzle types and difficulties
- Integration with educational and mathematics training
- Math puzzle creation tools for users
- Integration with competitive and learning modes
