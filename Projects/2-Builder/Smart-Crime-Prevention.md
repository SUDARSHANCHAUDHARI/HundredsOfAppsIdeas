# Smart Crime Prevention

## Description
An intelligent crime prevention platform that uses AI to optimize prevention strategies, track crime patterns, and provide personalized crime prevention insights through smart prevention tools.

## User Stories
- As a crime prevention enthusiast, I want AI-powered prevention optimization
- As a crime prevention specialist, I want to manage prevention strategies effectively
- As a law enforcement officer, I want to prevent crimes before they occur
- As a community leader, I want to implement effective crime prevention programs
- As a citizen, I want to contribute to crime prevention efforts

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered crime prevention optimization and insights
- Integration with crime prevention tools and law enforcement systems
- Personalized crime prevention experience tracking and analytics
- Social features for crime prevention communities
- Multiple crime prevention domains and strategies
- Integration with predictive analytics and risk assessment
- Crime prevention creation tools for organizations
- Integration with community policing and engagement
