# Intermediate Photo Gallery

## Description
An intermediate photo gallery application where users can upload, organize, and share photos with albums, tags, search functionality, and beautiful image displays.

## User Stories
- As a photographer, I want to showcase my photos
- As a user, I want to organize my photo collection
- As a social user, I want to share photos with friends
- As a developer, I want to build a photo platform

## Bonus Features
- Photo upload and management
- Album creation and organization
- Photo tagging and categorization
- Image search and filtering
- Photo sharing and social features
- Image editing and filters
- Slideshow and gallery views
- Responsive image display
