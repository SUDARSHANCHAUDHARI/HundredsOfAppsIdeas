# Mental Health Companion

## Description
An AI-powered mental health companion app that provides emotional support, mood tracking, meditation guidance, and connects users with mental health resources when needed.

## User Stories
- As a user, I want to track my mood and emotional patterns
- As a user, I want AI-powered chat support for emotional guidance
- As a user, I want personalized meditation and mindfulness exercises
- As a user, I want crisis intervention features when needed
- As a user, I want to connect with licensed therapists

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered sentiment analysis from journal entries
- Personalized mental health insights and recommendations
- Integration with wearables for stress monitoring
- Community support groups with anonymity
- Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) exercises
- Sleep quality tracking and improvement suggestions
- Breathing exercises with biofeedback
- Emergency contact and crisis hotline integration
