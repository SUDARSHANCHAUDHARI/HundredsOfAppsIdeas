# Smart Employee Engagement

## Description
An intelligent employee engagement platform that uses AI to optimize workplace satisfaction, track engagement levels, and provide personalized employee experiences through smart engagement tools.

## User Stories
- As an employee engagement enthusiast, I want AI-powered engagement optimization
- As an HR professional, I want to improve employee satisfaction effectively
- As an employee, I want to have a positive and engaging work experience
- As a manager, I want to enhance team engagement and morale
- As an executive, I want to optimize organizational engagement strategy

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered employee engagement optimization and insights
- Integration with satisfaction surveys and feedback systems
- Personalized employee experience tracking and analytics
- Social features for employee engagement communities
- Multiple engagement domains and improvement strategies
- Integration with HR systems and performance management
- Employee engagement creation tools for organizations
- Integration with wellness and work-life balance programs
