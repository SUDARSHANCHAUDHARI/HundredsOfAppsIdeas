# Smart Fitness Tracker

## Description
An advanced fitness tracking platform that uses AI to analyze workout performance, provide personalized training recommendations, and integrate with wearable devices for comprehensive health monitoring.

## User Stories
- As a fitness enthusiast, I want AI-powered workout optimization
- As an athlete, I want performance analysis and improvement suggestions
- As a beginner, I want personalized fitness guidance
- As a health-conscious person, I want comprehensive health tracking
- As a trainer, I want to monitor client progress effectively

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered workout optimization and personalization
- Integration with wearable devices and sensors
- Real-time performance monitoring and feedback
- Integration with nutrition and diet tracking
- Social features for fitness community engagement
- Integration with health and medical systems
- Predictive analytics for injury prevention
- Integration with fitness equipment and gyms

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with fitness tracking interface
- Backend: Python with AI and fitness analytics
- AI: TensorFlow for performance analysis and optimization
- Database: MongoDB with fitness and health data
- APIs: HealthKit, wearable device APIs
- Real-time: WebSocket for live performance tracking
- Analytics: Fitness performance and health metrics
- Integration: Wearable devices and health APIs
