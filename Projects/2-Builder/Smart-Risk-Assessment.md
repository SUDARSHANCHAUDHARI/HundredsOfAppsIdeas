# Smart Risk Assessment

## Description
An intelligent risk assessment platform that uses AI to analyze and evaluate risks, track risk patterns, and provide personalized risk insights through smart risk assessment tools.

## User Stories
- As a risk assessment enthusiast, I want AI-powered risk analysis
- As a risk manager, I want to assess and mitigate risks effectively
- As a compliance officer, I want to ensure regulatory compliance in risk management
- As a business owner, I want to identify and manage business risks
- As an investor, I want to assess investment risks and opportunities

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered risk assessment optimization and insights
- Integration with risk assessment tools and compliance systems
- Personalized risk assessment experience tracking and analytics
- Social features for risk assessment communities
- Multiple risk assessment domains and mitigation strategies
- Integration with predictive analytics and scenario modeling
- Risk assessment creation tools for organizations
- Integration with compliance and regulatory systems

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with risk assessment interface
- Backend: Python with AI and risk assessment processing
- AI: TensorFlow for risk assessment optimization
- Database: MongoDB with risk assessment metadata and analysis data
- Risk Engine: Custom risk assessment automation and monitoring
- Analytics: Risk patterns and mitigation tracking
- Integration: Risk assessment tools and predictive analytics APIs
- Social: Community features and risk assessment sharing
