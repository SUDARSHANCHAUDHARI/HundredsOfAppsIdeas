# Smart Talent Management

## Description
An intelligent talent management platform that uses AI to optimize employee development, track career progression, and provide personalized growth opportunities through smart talent management tools.

## User Stories
- As a talent management enthusiast, I want AI-powered talent optimization
- As an HR professional, I want to manage employee development effectively
- As an employee, I want personalized career growth opportunities
- As a manager, I want to support team member development
- As an executive, I want to optimize organizational talent strategy

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered talent management optimization and recommendations
- Integration with performance management and skill assessment
- Personalized talent development tracking and analytics
- Social features for talent management communities
- Multiple talent domains and development paths
- Integration with HR systems and professional development
- Talent management creation tools for organizations
- Integration with succession planning and leadership development
