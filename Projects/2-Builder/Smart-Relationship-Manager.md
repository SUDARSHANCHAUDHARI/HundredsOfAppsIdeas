# Smart Relationship Manager

## Description
An intelligent relationship management platform that uses AI to analyze communication patterns, suggest relationship improvements, and help users maintain meaningful connections with friends, family, and colleagues.

## User Stories
- As a user, I want AI to remind me to maintain important relationships
- As a busy professional, I want to track my social network and connections
- As a friend, I want to remember important dates and milestones
- As a networking professional, I want to optimize my relationship building
- As a family member, I want to strengthen family bonds through better communication

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered relationship strength analysis and suggestions
- Integration with social media and communication platforms
- Automated birthday and anniversary reminders
- Communication pattern analysis and insights
- Relationship health scoring and improvement recommendations
- Integration with calendar and task management
- Social network visualization and analysis
- Privacy-focused relationship data management
