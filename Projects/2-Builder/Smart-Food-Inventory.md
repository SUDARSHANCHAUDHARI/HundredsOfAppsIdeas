# Smart Food Inventory Manager

## Description
An intelligent food inventory management system that tracks groceries, suggests recipes based on available ingredients, and reduces food waste through expiration monitoring and smart shopping lists.

## User Stories
- As a home cook, I want to track my food inventory and expiration dates
- As a budget-conscious shopper, I want to reduce food waste and save money
- As a busy parent, I want meal suggestions based on available ingredients
- As a health-conscious person, I want to track nutritional content
- As a grocery shopper, I want automated shopping lists based on inventory

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered recipe suggestions based on available ingredients
- Barcode scanning for easy inventory updates
- Integration with grocery delivery services
- Nutritional tracking and health insights
- Food waste analytics and cost savings reports
- Integration with smart refrigerators and pantries
- Meal planning and preparation scheduling
- Community recipe sharing and rating system

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with inventory management interface
- Backend: Node.js with recipe and nutrition APIs
- AI: TensorFlow for recipe recommendation engine
- Database: MongoDB with food inventory data
- APIs: Edamam, Nutritionix, barcode APIs
- Mobile: React Native with barcode scanning
- Integration: Smart refrigerator APIs
- Notifications: Expiration date alerts
