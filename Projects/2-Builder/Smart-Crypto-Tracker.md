# Smart Cryptocurrency Tracker

## Description
An advanced cryptocurrency tracking platform that uses AI to analyze market trends, predict price movements, and provide intelligent trading insights for cryptocurrency investors.

## User Stories
- As a crypto investor, I want AI-powered market analysis and predictions
- As a trader, I want real-time alerts and trading signals
- As a portfolio manager, I want to optimize my crypto portfolio
- As a researcher, I want to analyze blockchain data and trends
- As a beginner, I want to learn about cryptocurrency investing

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered market trend analysis and prediction
- Integration with multiple exchanges and wallets
- Real-time price alerts and notifications
- Portfolio optimization and rebalancing suggestions
- Integration with DeFi protocols and yield farming
- Social trading and copy trading features
- Risk assessment and management tools
- Integration with tax reporting services

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with crypto dashboard
- Backend: Python with AI and blockchain analytics
- AI: TensorFlow for market prediction and analysis
- Database: MongoDB with crypto data and portfolios
- APIs: CoinGecko, Binance, and exchange APIs
- Real-time: WebSocket for live price updates
- Blockchain: Web3.js for blockchain integration
- Integration: DeFi and tax reporting APIs
