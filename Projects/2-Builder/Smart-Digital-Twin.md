# Smart Digital Twin Platform

## Description
A comprehensive digital twin platform that creates virtual replicas of physical objects, systems, or processes with real-time data synchronization and predictive analytics for optimization and monitoring.

## User Stories
- As an engineer, I want to create digital twins of physical systems
- As a manufacturer, I want to monitor and optimize production processes
- As a city planner, I want to simulate urban systems and infrastructure
- As a facility manager, I want to optimize building operations
- As a researcher, I want to study complex systems through simulation

## Bonus Features
- Real-time data synchronization with physical systems
- AI-powered predictive analytics and optimization
- Integration with IoT sensors and monitoring systems
- Advanced simulation and modeling capabilities
- Multi-scale digital twin creation (component to system level)
- Integration with augmented reality for visualization
- Historical data analysis and trend prediction
- Collaborative digital twin development and sharing

## Technologies to Consider
- Frontend: React with 3D visualization and AR interfaces
- Backend: Python with simulation and analytics
- AI: TensorFlow for predictive analytics and optimization
- Database: Time-series databases for real-time data
- IoT: Integration with sensor networks and monitoring systems
- AR/VR: Augmented reality for digital twin visualization
- Simulation: Advanced physics and system modeling
- Real-time: WebSocket for live data synchronization
