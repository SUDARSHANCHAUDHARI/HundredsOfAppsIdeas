# Smart Home Energy Monitor

## Description
A comprehensive energy monitoring dashboard that tracks, analyzes, and optimizes smart home energy consumption with real-time data visualization and AI-powered recommendations.

## User Stories
- As a homeowner, I want to monitor real-time energy consumption of all devices
- As a homeowner, I want AI to suggest energy-saving optimizations
- As a homeowner, I want to set energy budgets and receive alerts
- As a homeowner, I want to compare my usage with similar homes
- As a homeowner, I want to control devices remotely to save energy

## Bonus Features
- Integration with smart home devices (Nest, Philips Hue, Smart Plugs)
- Predictive energy usage forecasting
- Solar panel integration and monitoring
- Time-of-use pricing optimization
- Carbon footprint tracking
- Voice control integration (Alexa, Google Assistant)
- Mobile app with push notifications
- Historical data analysis and trends
