# Personal Productivity Coach

## Description
An AI-powered personal productivity coach that analyzes work patterns, suggests optimizations, and helps users build better habits through intelligent time management and focus enhancement.

## User Stories
- As a professional, I want AI to analyze my productivity patterns
- As a student, I want personalized study schedule optimization
- As a remote worker, I want focus enhancement and distraction blocking
- As a team member, I want to improve meeting efficiency
- As a goal-setter, I want habit tracking and achievement motivation

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered productivity pattern analysis
- Integration with calendar and task management tools
- Focus music and ambient sound optimization
- Eye strain and break reminders
- Meeting preparation and follow-up automation
- Energy level tracking and task scheduling
- Social accountability features
- Integration with wearable devices for health monitoring
