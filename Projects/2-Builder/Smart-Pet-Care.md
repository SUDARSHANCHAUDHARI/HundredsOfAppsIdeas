# Smart Pet Care System

## Description
A comprehensive pet care management platform that tracks pet health, activity, and behavior using IoT devices and AI-powered insights for optimal pet wellness.

## User Stories
- As a pet owner, I want to monitor my pet's health and activity levels
- As a veterinarian, I want to track patient health between visits
- As a pet sitter, I want to access pet care instructions and schedules
- As a pet trainer, I want to track training progress and behavior patterns
- As a pet food company, I want to recommend nutrition based on pet data

## Bonus Features
- AI-powered behavior analysis and training recommendations
- Integration with smart pet feeders and water dispensers
- GPS tracking and virtual fence for outdoor pets
- Health trend analysis and early illness detection
- Integration with veterinary clinics and emergency services
- Pet social network and playdate matching
- Nutrition optimization based on pet characteristics
- Multi-pet household management
